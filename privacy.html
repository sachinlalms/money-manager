Privacy Policy

Money Manager · Last updated: May 2025
Your data never touches our servers

1. Overview

Money Manager is a personal finance tracking app. We do not collect, store, or access your personal or financial data in any way.

2. Data storage

All your data is stored in one of two places, both fully under your control:

  • Your device's local storage — without signing in, all data stays in your browser only.
  • Your own Google Drive — when you sign in with Google, the app creates a Sheet called "Money Manager" in your Drive. Only you can access it. The app owner has zero access.

3. Google account access

When you sign in with Google, the app requests these permissions:

  • drive.file — create and edit only the files this app creates in your Drive. No access to any other Drive files.
  • spreadsheets — read and write the Money Manager Sheet in your Drive.

No data is sent to any server owned by the app developer.

4. What we do not collect

  • No personal information is stored on our servers.
  • No OAuth tokens are stored on any server — only in your browser's localStorage.
  • No cookies, analytics, advertising trackers, or third-party monitoring.
  • No data sharing with third parties.

5. AI features

Optional AI features send a summary of your financial data (totals, categories, recent transaction descriptions) to the Anthropic Claude API only when you explicitly tap an AI button. Please review Anthropic's privacy policy for details.
